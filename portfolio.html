Not much to show off at the moment. While I continue to develop workproducts, please enjoy some photos of my first foster doggo, Kira. She is a 4 years old Shelttie-mix who traveled from Texas. She was adopted out to a wonderful family living in Michigan.

Hello, look at my pretty fur. Orange you glad just looking at me?

Very chic in purple.

When she came to us, she was timid and pretty nervous about going on walks. We bonded through leash training (although, I think it was more about the hotdogs she got as rewards that really won her over).

My favorite food is chicken, beef, rice with chicken, beef chunks, dried jerky, sometimes sweet potatoes or pumpkin, but gimme chicken!!

Kira isn't the biggest fan of water, so we got her a rain jacket to help with the rainy spring days. Kira thinks putting on clothing means play time, even though human only wants to get serious!

Watching good ole' foster-dad dirt bike and wheelie -- he was SO good! Kira didn't mind the loud noises as long as got cuddles in between rides :)

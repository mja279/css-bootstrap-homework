About Me

Get to Know Me

I was born in Mar del Plata, a coastal town in the province of Buenos Aires, Argentina. From the time I was 6 years-old, I was raised in the Washington-Metropolitan area. After graduating from college and starting my full-time job, I relocated to the Detroit-Metropolitan area where I currently reside. I love to enage in activities that challenge me to think creatively and problem-solve. From a young age, I've loved building puzzles and painting. If I decided to pursue a completely different career than that which I currently am in (i.e. project management in tech) I would strive to the world's best veterinary so I could work alongside man's best friends!
